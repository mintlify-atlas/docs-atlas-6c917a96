## Introduction

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone involved in the **Inspira UI** project. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for participant behavior as well as the consequences for unacceptable conduct.

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and inclusive community, we pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card icon="user">
    Age, body size, and personal appearance
  </Card>
  <Card icon="wheelchair">
    Disability and level of experience
  </Card>
  <Card icon="globe">
    Ethnicity, nationality, and race
  </Card>
  <Card icon="heart">
    Gender identity, expression, and sexual orientation
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Expected Behavior

All participants in our community are expected to:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Be Respectful">
    Show empathy and kindness towards others. Treat everyone with dignity and respect.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Be Considerate">
    Remember that your actions and words affect others. Think before you speak or act.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Be Collaborative">
    Work together to achieve common goals. Share knowledge and help others grow.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Communicate Effectively">
    Use clear and constructive language. Be open to feedback and willing to learn.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Demonstrate Professionalism">
    Act professionally and take responsibility for your actions. Be accountable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable within our community:

<Warning>
  **Harassment and Discrimination**
  
  Including derogatory comments, slurs, or unwanted sexual attention.
</Warning>

<Warning>
  **Abuse and Threats**
  
  Any form of verbal or written abuse, intimidation, or threats.
</Warning>

<Warning>
  **Trolling and Insults**
  
  Provocative or insulting remarks intended to disrupt conversations.
</Warning>

<Warning>
  **Disrespectful Communication**
  
  Including excessive profanity, shouting (using all caps), or interrupting others.
</Warning>

<Warning>
  **Personal Attacks**
  
  Targeting an individual with the intent to harass or belittle.
</Warning>

## Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns, please report it as soon as possible by contacting the project maintainers on our **Discord channel**:

<Card title="Report an Incident" icon="discord" href="https://discord.gg/Xbh5DwJRc9">
  Join our Discord channel to report incidents or contact maintainers.
</Card>

### When Reporting an Incident

Please include:

- **Your Contact Information**: Your Discord username or any preferred method of contact
- **Names of Those Involved**: Real names or usernames of the individuals involved
- **Description of the Incident**: A clear and concise account of what happened
- **Supporting Evidence**: Any relevant messages, screenshots, or context that can help us understand the situation

<Note>
  All reports will be handled confidentially.
</Note>

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for ensuring compliance with this Code of Conduct and will take appropriate action in response to any behavior that is deemed unacceptable.

Actions may include:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Warning">
    A private warning to the offender about their behavior.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Temporary Ban">
    Temporary suspension from participation in the project and Discord channel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Permanent Ban">
    Permanent ban from participation in the project and Discord channel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Content Removal">
    Removal of contributions that violate the Code of Conduct.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Appeal Process

<Info>
  Any individual who is subjected to disciplinary action has the right to appeal the decision.
</Info>

To appeal:

1. Contact the project maintainers through the **Discord channel**
2. Submit your appeal within **one week** of the action
3. The appeal will be reviewed
4. A final decision will be communicated to you

## Privacy

All reports of unacceptable behavior will be handled with discretion. We will respect the privacy of the reporter and the accused.
